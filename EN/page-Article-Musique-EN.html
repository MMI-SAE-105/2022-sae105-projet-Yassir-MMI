The victory anthem of the 2018 World Cup ?

This music was composed by the artist Vegedream, and was released on 18 July 2018 just on the night of the victory. Accumulating nearly 60 million views on the YouTube platform, it became the anthem of this victory using a repetitive rhythm accompanied by an Afro style.

This musical phenomenon was originally written by Vegedream and was dedicated mainly to his friends in the French team Umtiti, Kimpembe and Mendy. When the French won, Vegedream sent it to Mendy, where the success started to build. He is often criticized for only mentioning his friends in the lyrics, but the artist defends himself by explaining that the song was written primarily for his close friends but also for the whole team, yet even Didier Deschamps is mentioned! The song is currently certified "platinum" and was sung by this emerging singer at the Stade de France in front of an audience of 90,000 people. Its first publication was a leaked extract that quickly went viral on the networks.


A Hit music not planned... ?

We can see that almost all of the lyrics identify a specific player, this music was originally intended to thank certain players, but soon after it became a national hit for an unknown rapper.


We can now say that it is officially the anthem of this victory, since it was sung at the Stade de France during the inauguration of this world cup, just as Gloria Gaynor's song "I will Survive" was after the victory in 1998.
