Glossary

MMI Department :

The Multimedia and Internet Professions department is the training department of this BUT of the same name. They train future experts in the fields of communication, computer graphics, multimedia creation, web development and digital technologies.


Mercato :

In football, the mercato refers to the official transfer market from club to player for a limited period of time.


Golden Ball :

The Golden Ball is, for every football fan, the individual award with the greatest reputation, It grants the players who hold it an outstanding record of achievement
